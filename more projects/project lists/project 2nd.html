Project Overview

"Pro Catcher" is an exciting arcade game that challenges players to catch various mechanical parts while avoiding obstacles. With each part collected, players score points, aiming to rack up high scores without losing lives or missing parts.

Key Features

  • Unique Parts: Different mechanical parts, each with its own appearance and point value, add variety to the gameplay.
  • Dynamic Levels: Each level grows progressively harder, keeping players on their toes.
  • Score & Health Display: A real-time scoreboard tracks your score, lives, and missed parts.
  • Gradual Difficulty Scaling: The game ramps up in difficulty smoothly, making it engaging as you progress.
  • Intuitive Controls: Simple, responsive controls let players focus on catching parts and enjoying the game.

Challenges Faced

  • Physics Optimization: Making falling parts move naturally required careful adjustments.
  • Balancing Gameplay: Designing a fair scoring and health system took some fine-tuning.
  • UI Design: Crafting a visually appealing, dark-themed interface took multiple iterations.
  • Collision Accuracy: Ensuring precise collisions between the catcher and parts to avoid unfair misses.

Game Impact

It boosts reflexes and concentration while giving a fun introduction to mechanical concepts. It’s a game that’s not only entertaining but also subtly educational, appealing to casual gamers and future engineers alike.

Final Thoughts

With its unique mechanics-based theme and engaging gameplay, "Pro Catcher" is fun for all ages. The game has room for future updates, like adding new parts, power-ups, or even a multiplayer mode, to keep players coming back for more.
